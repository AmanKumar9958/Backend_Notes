Framework: It gives us flow to done our work, it means it will gives us steps and we must have to follow all the steps.
Library: It provide us tools to complete our work.

Express JS is a framework.
It is a NPM package.
It manages everything from receiving the request and giving the response, connecting to databases, etc.
To use Express in our project: 
    To install: 'npm i express'
    To run: 'node fileName'

To see changes we have to manually restart the server, we makes create frustration, to solve this we can use nodemon, here are te steps:
    Install: 'npm i nodemon -g' (g for global installation)
